Cookies Policy

Cookies aren’t just tasty treats — they’re also tiny text files stored on your device that help us understand how you use our site and how we can make it better.

What Cookies We Use

We may use:

  • Analytics cookies (via Google Analytics) to see what pages are poppin’
  • Performance cookies to make the site smoother and faster
  • Functional cookies to remember things like your preferences

Why We Use Cookies

  • To improve the site’s performance
  • To know what content people like
  • To help us make your experience better (less guesswork, more wow)

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings — delete them, block them, or allow only certain ones. If you choose to block them, some parts of the site might not function as intended.
