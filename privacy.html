Privacy Policy

At Viora Visuals, we take your privacy seriously — not because we have to say that, but because we’re humans too. Here's everything you need to know about how we handle your information.

What Information We Collect

We only collect what we need, and nothing extra. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Any info you give us via contact forms, emails, or messages
  • Browsing data via cookies or Google Analytics

How We Use Your Info

  • To contact you about services or collaborations
  • To respond to inquiries
  • To improve our site experience (analytics)
  • Occasionally to follow up (if you asked us to)

Sharing Your Data

Nope — we don’t sell, trade, or rent your data to others. Your info stays with us, or with safe third-party platforms we use (like Google Workspace or a form tool), strictly for internal use.

How Long We Keep Your Data

You can ask:

  • What data we have on you
  • For us to update, correct, or delete your data Just email us and we’ll sort it out!

Security Measures

We use standard encryption and secure platforms to keep things locked down. While no system is 100% hack-proof, we do everything we can to keep your data safe.
